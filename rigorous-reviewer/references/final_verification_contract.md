# Final Verification Contract

Before delivery, verify:

- [ ] Central claim is reconstructed.
- [ ] Missing decisive materials are listed.
- [ ] Manuscript-internal and external evidence are separated.
- [ ] Critical/Major issues contain full logic chains.
- [ ] Every issue maps to a claim, figure, method, proof, dataset, benchmark, or standard.
- [ ] No fabricated citations, identifiers, parameters, or statistics.
- [ ] Search hints are decision-changing rather than generic.
- [ ] Recommendation follows unresolved claim dependency and revision feasibility.
- [ ] No casual summary language replaces rigorous review.
