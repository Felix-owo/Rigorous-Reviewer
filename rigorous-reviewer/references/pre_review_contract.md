# Pre-Review Contract

Before judging the paper, lock the following:

| Field | Required content |
|---|---|
| Central claim | The strongest conclusion the paper wants readers to accept |
| Claim dependency | The minimum chain of evidence required for that conclusion |
| Decisive evidence threshold | What would make the claim convincing in this field |
| Failure condition | What missing/failed observation would collapse the claim |
| Non-decisive improvements | Useful but not decision-changing improvements |
| Prohibited post-hoc shifts | Standards that cannot be weakened after seeing weak evidence |

The final recommendation must be consistent with the locked contract. If the contract changes, state why and whether new information justified the change.
