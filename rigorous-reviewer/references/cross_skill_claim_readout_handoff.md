# Cross-Skill Claim-Readout Handoff

Use this when a manuscript claim depends on protocol-derived evidence.

| Manuscript claim | Required readout | Protocol step | QC gate | Failure mode | Impact on claim | Needed BPR support |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Rigorous Reviewer owns the final scientific recommendation. Biological Protocol Reviewer may support protocol readiness, readout validity, QC gates, and SOP execution risk. It does not decide manuscript acceptance.
