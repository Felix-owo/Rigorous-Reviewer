# Reviewer Panel Protocol

Run these passes before final synthesis:

1. **Editor-in-Chief:** novelty, journal fit, decision threshold, field impact.
2. **Methods/Statistics/Proof Reviewer:** design, controls, experimental unit, statistical validity, proof completeness, benchmark validity.
3. **Domain Expert:** field-specific plausibility, missing controls, prior work, biological/chemical/physical/medical/computational standards.
4. **Interdisciplinary Reviewer:** bridge-claim validity across domains.
5. **Devil's Advocate:** strongest alternative explanation and failure mode.
6. **Synthesis Reviewer:** severity ranking, revision feasibility, final recommendation.

Do not collapse these passes into generic bullet points. The synthesis may be concise, but it must preserve disagreements and decision-changing risks.
