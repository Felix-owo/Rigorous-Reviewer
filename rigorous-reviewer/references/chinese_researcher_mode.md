# Chinese Researcher Mode

When writing in Chinese:

- Keep scientific terms precise; use English terms for ambiguous concepts such as `readout`, `benchmark`, `lineage tracing`, `batch effect`, `orthogonal validation`.
- Avoid inflated rhetorical praise or generic “建议进一步验证”.
- Translate reviewer severity into action-oriented laboratory or manuscript revision language.
- Use full issue blocks, not short comments.
